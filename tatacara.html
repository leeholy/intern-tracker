Tata Cara Pengaktifan Akaun Google UiTM
(Google UiTM Account Activation Guide)

Bahasa Melayu

  1. Buka laman web: https://mail.uitm.edu.my/student-guide
  2. Masukkan ID Pelajar anda (No. Matrik / No. Pelajar).
  3. Tekan butang "Semak".
  4. Maklumat akaun Google UiTM anda akan dipaparkan (alamat emel dan kata laluan sementara).
  5. Login ke Gmail menggunakan maklumat tersebut.
  6. Tukar kata laluan anda selepas log masuk pertama kali.
  7. Jika anda menghadapi masalah, hubungi Unit ICT Fakulti atau HEA / HEP UiTM kampus anda.

English

  1. Visit the website: https://mail.uitm.edu.my/student-guide
  2. Enter your Student ID (Matric Number).
  3. Click the "Check" button.
  4. Your Google UiTM account information will be displayed (email address and temporary password).
  5. Login to Gmail using those credentials.
  6. Change your password after the first login.
